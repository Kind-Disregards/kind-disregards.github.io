Hydenburg

In a rolling tide of pulsing beats, drips, and bubbles, Hydenburg is the underwater rave cheering you along your quest into a surreal world between sleep and awake. It's the mischievous voice in your head persuading you to do something, somewhere, for some reason.

The single was mixed by Christian Carrière and mastered by Bill Skibbe (Jack White, Black Keys).
Artwork by Louis-Ernest Bois.

Photo: Victoria Ho

Kind Disregards is a Toronto-Montréal collaboration between Christian Carrière (Mutek) and Pascal Delaquis (Little Scream, Elisapie). Pandemic-era call-and-response compositions sparked initial interest. But when Delaquis and Carrière finally played together in person in the summer of 2022, their palpable chemistry sparked the motto: ‘The first take is the last take‘.
