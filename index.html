Kind Disregards

DEBUT SINGLE

- Hydenburg -

In a rolling tide of drones, drips, and sonic bubbles, Hydenburg is the underwater rave cheering you along your quest into a surreal world between asleep and awake. It's the mischievous voice in your head enticing you to do something ... somewhere.

The single was mastered by Bill Skibbe (Jack White, Black Keys).
Artwork by Louis-Ernest Bois.

Available on all platforms April 26, 2024

Photo: V.Ho

Kind Disregards is a long-distance Toronto-Montréal collaboration between drummer Pascal Delaquis (Little Scream, Elisapie) and synthesist Christian Carrière (Mutek). Born out of a process of call-and-response composition, the project evolved by embracing the raw energy that animates inital impulses. "Don't overthink it!"
