Kind Disregards

DEBUT SINGLE

- Hydenburg -

In a rolling tide of drones, drips, and sonic bubbles, Hydenburg is the underwater rave cheering you along your quest into a surreal world between asleep and awake. It's the mischievous voice in your head enticing you to do something ... somewhere.

The single was mastered by Bill Skibbe (Jack White, Black Keys).
Artwork by Louis-Ernest Bois.

Available on all platforms April 26, 2024

Photo: V.Ho

Kind Disregards is a long-distance collaboration between multi-instrumentalists Christian Carrière (Montréal) and Pascal Delaquis (Toronto). Borne out of a process of call-and-response composition, the project evolved by embracing the raw energy that animates inital impulses. "Don't overthink it!"

At his core, Pascal is a drummer with a passion for percussive textures and deep grooves. He learned on pots and pans; a practice that has evolved into his present-day tone and sound inquiries. He has collaborated on acclaimed studio albums, including Little Scream’s Speed Queen (Merge Records). On the road, Delaquis toured extensively with Juno award-winning Elisapie across North America and Europe.

Christian is a sound artist and musician who’s work lives in the dreamy grey zone between sound design, soundscape and music. His pieces have been broadcast on CBC, BBC3 and Radio-Canada, among others. He has released several albums and singles, both as a solo artist and with collaborators. His sound installations have been shown in galleries and museums in Canada, the UK, Denmark, and Turkey.
